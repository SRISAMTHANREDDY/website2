Microwave Photonic Radar

Abstract: Over the years there has been increasing need for Identification, Sensing and Tracking systems for automobiles, air, marine and extra-terrestrial traffic for both public and defence applications. As a feasible solution this prospect proposes IST applications with Microwave photonics enabled Radar. Further with the advantage of Microwave Photonics the proposed system has high Bandwidth, less form factor. Considering the relative scan distance required at X-band (10 GHz) and K-band (22 GHz) were chosen. Frequency upscaling and downscaling are done using techniques like photonic frequency multiplying and photonic frequency quadrupling enabling the reconfigurability (X and K bands) of the system depending on the objects relative scan distance and RCS characteristics. The carrier is a Linear Frequency Modulated Continuous Wave (LFMCW) and the whole analysis is carried out using MATLAB Simulations.
